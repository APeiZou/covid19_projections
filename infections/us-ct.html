Estimates generated using confirmed cases and testing data from The COVID Tracking Project. Unlike the previous iteration of our model (Apr-Oct), no death data is used. We are working to put together a write-up of the methodology.

Note: Due to the inherent lag in reporting of cases, we do not have estimates of infections for the last 10 days.
Note #2: For this project, we are nowcasting (what has happened) rather than forecasting (what will happen). See our blog for alternative models that have forecasts for cases and deaths.
